3

4
Tool: query_database

5
Tool: generate_report
Synthesize findings ‚Üí Create formatted report with: drivers list, trends, risk assessment, recommendations
6
Final Response
Present comprehensive analysis: "Found 12 drivers with break violations. 3 show increasing trend. 2 are approaching HOS limits. Recommendations: [specific actions]"
